Last updated: {{ moment().format('MMMM Do, YYYY') }}

Your Privacy Matters: This Privacy Policy explains how Comolor POS collects, uses, and protects your personal information and business data.

1. Introduction

Comolor POS ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Point of Sale service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, business name
  • Business Information: Business type, address, tax information
  • Payment Information: M-Pesa phone numbers, transaction records
  • Contact Information: Communication preferences, support requests

2.2 Business Data

When you use our Service, we collect and store your business data, including:

  • Product catalogs and inventory information
  • Customer records and transaction history
  • Sales data and financial records
  • Staff information and access logs
  • Business settings and preferences

2.3 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • IP addresses and device information
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Usage patterns and feature utilization
  • Log files and error reports
  • Performance and analytics data

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Comolor POS service
  • Process transactions and payments
  • Generate reports and analytics
  • Manage user accounts and permissions
  • Provide customer support

3.2 Communication

We may use your contact information to:

  • Send service updates and notifications
  • Provide license expiry warnings
  • Respond to support requests
  • Send important security alerts
  • Share product updates and improvements

3.3 Legal Compliance

We may use your information to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Respond to lawful requests from authorities
  • Protect our rights and interests
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Third-Party Services

We may share your information with trusted third-party services:

  • M-Pesa/Safaricom: For payment processing and transaction verification
  • Cloud Providers: For secure data storage and backup
  • Analytics Services: For service improvement and monitoring
  • Support Tools: For customer service and technical support

4.2 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business transaction.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests from public authorities.

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited access based on role and necessity
  • Authentication: Secure login and session management
  • Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for security threats
  • Auditing: Regular security audits and assessments

5.2 Data Backup

We perform regular automated backups of your data to ensure business continuity and disaster recovery.

5.3 Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities in accordance with applicable laws.

6. Data Retention

6.1 Active Accounts

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the Service.

6.2 Terminated Accounts

After account termination, we may retain your data for up to 12 months to comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

6.3 Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your data by contacting our support team. Some data may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes.

7. Your Rights

7.1 Access Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request copies of your data
  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Export your business data

7.2 Control Rights

You can:

  • Modify your communication preferences
  • Update your account information
  • Manage user access and permissions
  • Request account deactivation

7.3 Data Portability

You have the right to export your data in a commonly used format if you choose to migrate to another service.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored and processed in Kenya. If we transfer data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

10. Cookies and Tracking

10.1 Essential Cookies

We use essential cookies necessary for the Service to function properly, including:

  • Session management cookies
  • Authentication cookies
  • Security cookies

10.2 Analytics

We may use analytics tools to understand how the Service is used and to improve user experience.

11. Third-Party Links

Our Service may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

12. Regional Compliance

12.1 Kenya Data Protection

We comply with the Kenya Data Protection Act and other applicable local data protection laws.

12.2 International Standards

We follow international best practices for data protection and privacy, including ISO 27001 guidelines.

13. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Data Protection Officer
  • Email: privacy@comolor.com
  • Phone: +254 700 000 000
  • Address: Nairobi, Kenya
  • Support Hours: Monday - Friday, 8 AM - 6 PM (GMT+3)

14. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Sending an email notification
  • Posting a notice in the Service
  • Updating the "Last updated" date above

15. Your Consent

By using our Service, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Commitment: We are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the security of your business data. Your trust is essential to our service.
Questions? If you have any questions about how we handle your data, please don't hesitate to contact our Data Protection Officer using the information provided above.
