Our Story

Comolor POS was born from a simple observation: many Kenyan businesses were struggling with outdated, expensive, or overly complex point-of-sale systems that didn't understand the local market needs.

Founded in 2024, we set out to create a solution that would be:

  • Affordable: Reasonable pricing for businesses of all sizes
  • Local: Built with Kenyan businesses in mind
  • Integrated: Seamless M-Pesa integration for modern payments
  • User-friendly: Easy to learn and use for any business owner

Our Vision

We envision a Kenya where every business, from the smallest shop to the largest retail chain, has access to modern, efficient point-of-sale technology that helps them grow and serve their customers better.

By 2025, we aim to:

  • Serve over 10,000 businesses across Kenya
  • Process millions of transactions monthly
  • Become the preferred POS solution for SMEs
  • Expand to other East African markets
